What is The Campfire?

Campfire is a newsletter from Lewis, a direct line into what's actually happening at Camprowe, before it shows up anywhere else.

Everything public-facing, the videos, the breakdowns, the finished work, is the result of a lot that happens before that. Campfire is where that part lives. What's in progress, what's working, what's been scrapped, what's coming next across Geekotist, Patch Notes, and Camprowe Audio. If you've ever wondered what goes into building something like this, this is where you'd find out.

It's also the first place new productions, formats, or projects get mentioned, often before there's anything public to point to yet. If you want to be ahead of what Camprowe becomes rather than catching up with it later, this is how. It's just Lewis, writing about what's actually going on, sent straight to people who want to follow along.
