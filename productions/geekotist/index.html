What is Geekotist?

A Geekotist is a know-it-all, someone who can't help but share all the little details of games, movies, TV and Comics.

Geekotist is where Camprowe makes video essays about the worlds you can't stop thinking about, the who, what, where, and when behind your favourite games, film, and TV.

Every world worth getting lost in is built from thousands of small decisions, lore that ties back further than you'd expect, details placed on purpose, stories hiding in plain sight. Geekotist exists to surface those pieces and share them properly: not as trivia, but as the reason these worlds get under your skin in the first place. At its core, this is a celebration. Of the writers, designers, and artists who put in the work. Of the worlds they built. And of the audience who noticed enough to go looking for more.

If you've ever finished a game or a show and immediately wanted to know more, more lore, more context, more of what made it work, Geekotist is built for you. New video essays drop regularly. Watch the latest, or catch up on the archive below.
